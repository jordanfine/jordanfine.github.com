About me

Hey! My name is Jordan Fine. I’m currently going into my fourth year at the University of Florida, studying Digital Arts & Sciences Engineering with a minor in Mass Communications.

Beyond my engineering curriculum, you’ll find me designing marketing materials for Reitz Union Board Entertainment. As Lead Marketing Designer, I create fliers and videos for the student run entertainment board that plans free events 7 days a week. Check us out!

When I’m not fiddling around with Illustrator and coding, I like to enjoy a nice cup of coffee and a movie. Around Gainesville, you’ll find me searching for that perfect slice of pizza, hanging out at cafes, catching a show at the Atlantic or enjoying a new, arthouse film at the Hippodrome Theatre.

After college, I hope to build off my experiences and passions as a Front End Developer in the tech industry.

I'd love to chat!
